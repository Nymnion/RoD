How to Play

  • Type !join to join the game during the joining phase
  • When in the cave, type !continue to explore deeper
  • Or type !exit to leave with your treasures
  • Be careful! If the same trap appears twice, everyone still in the cave loses all treasures
  • The game lasts 5 rounds - player with the most rubies wins!
